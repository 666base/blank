# Blank Workspace Guidelines

## Apple-like Quality Standard
- **No Bugging, Lagging, or Errors**: Strive for pure quality. Write defensive, reliable code. Prevent crashes and freezes proactively by handling edge cases.
- **Premium UX/UI**: Ensure every interface is clean and polished. Use proper transitions, spacing, and micro-interactions that match the premium mood of the app.
- **Simplicity**: Do not overdo or complicate things. Do not overthink or over-engineer. The best code is simple, elegant, and maintainable.
- **Data-Driven**: Do not guess blindly. Use facts, established use cases, and solid engineering principles to inform your decisions.
- **Performance**: Always optimize for speed. Ensure no lagging or freezing occurs, especially on mobile or during complex operations.

When you write or modify code in this workspace, hold it to these strict quality standards to ensure users have an impeccable experience.
